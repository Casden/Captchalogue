Fonts here are categorized as such:

- /handwriting: fonts made by Homestuck fans to emulate Hussie's hand-drawn text when writing as a Homestuck character, or from the spinoff games
- /uncategorized: fonts from Hussie's fonts folder that may or may not have been used in the comic proper (let us know via https://homestuck.net/contact.html if you see them!)
- main folder: fonts that we've confirmed are used in official material, alongside their use

Fonts not included in this archive due to easy availability or litigious owners include (https://mspaintadventures.fandom.com/wiki/Fonts):
- Courier New and Courier: used all over the comic
- Verdana: comic interface
- Comic Sans: SBAHJ et all
- Lucida Console: aliased text in GameFAQs and SBURB UI
- An All Star: the Oblivion font is the source of the old alternian fonts here, just upside down
- Times New Roman: various places, like the Sassacre book cover
- Arial Bold: various places, like the Common Hornographer newspaper
- Papyrus: This Ocean, Charles book cover
- Impact Bold: Data Structures for Assholes book cover
